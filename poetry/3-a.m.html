3 a.m

In the third of the night,
I whisper your name to God,
"If this isn’t right,
take it out of my heart."

But it stays…
soft under silver light.
It stays
every single night.

You only know my name,
maybe just my face.
You wouldn’t notice if I'm gone,
but I see you in the afterglow.

I tell God about you,
then let faith decide our way.
I leave the rest to fate,
no need to rush the days.

You make me want to change,
to be better in my own way.

If we’re meant to cross again,
we’ll find it someday.
So I let faith decide…
and hope in silence.
