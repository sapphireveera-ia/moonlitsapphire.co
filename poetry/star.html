a quite star.

Five months from now, I never knew
A star like you was always there
I didn’t see it till the moon
Spoke your name inside my sleep.

Since that night your smile stays
So softly curved inside my head.

Your eyes look like crescent moons
When you laugh, I lose myself
At two a.m. I ask the moon,
Will you look at me the way I look at you?

This feeling stays unspoken, still
I’m scared our worlds don’t face the same
But every night you cross my mind
And I just smile here in the dark.

Your eyes look like crescent moons
When you laugh, I lose myself
At two a.m. I ask the moon,
Will you look at me the way I look at you?

Hope is heavy in my chest
One day it might break in two
I just wish that you would know
You’re the quiet star I chose.

I want that gentle star to fall
Into my arms.
