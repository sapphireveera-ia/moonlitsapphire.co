quitely.

I never planned to write about you
yet somehow your name
keeps finding its way
between my lines.

You appear in quiet moments
like a thought I didn’t invite,
softly living somewhere
between my days and nights.

I never meant to feel this way,
never meant to let it grow,
but somehow liking you in silence
became something beautiful.

Maybe you will never notice,
maybe you will never see
how simply knowing you existed
changed a part of me.

I don’t know where life will take us,
maybe far from where we are,
maybe someday our two stories
meet again beneath the stars.

And if that moment ever finds us
somewhere in the future’s light,
I hope we meet again
as better hearts,
better souls,
better lives.
